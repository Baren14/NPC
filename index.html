About Us

NPC aims to provide a better note-taking experience with easy access to calculations

We Are NPC

In the past, we have noticed that it is difficult and cumbersome to use notepad as a platform for financial purposes as it lacks a calculator to assist users in calculation. Therefore, the idea of NotePad with Calculator (NPC) is coming here to solve the problem. As its name stated, we believe that NPC can lead you to a better experience while using it as a tool for your financial record as it provides a calculator for you to work on. It is very simple and easy to use.
